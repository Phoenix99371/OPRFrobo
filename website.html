About Robotics Club

OUR CLUB

Robotics is a student-led club and a chance to improve STEM skills in a collaborative environment. We offer a unique mix of engineering, technical abilities, computer science, leadership, teamwork, and competition; Club members work in teams of 4-6 to design, build, and operate robots. Varsity teams then pit their robots against other schools while Junior Varsity teams compete in-house.

MEETING SCHEDULE

Currently, Robotics Club meets Wednesdays and Thursdays at 3:20 in room 1297. Varsity team members must come to every meeting; JV members are welcome to attend as often as they are able.

ABOUT VEX

Our Varsity Robotics teams take their robots to compete in VEX tournaments. Each year, VEX designs a game, sells parts us to build with, and organizes tournaments for teams from different schools to compete in. The winners of these local competitions continue to State and Worlds. Over 20,000 teams from 400 countries are part of VEX. To learn more click here.

TIPPING POINT

The 2021-2022 VEX tournament game is Tipping Point. Robots must place rings on layered movable goals and can earn extra points by ending the match on an elevated platform. Click here to learn more about Tipping Point Up, or here to read the game manual. Be sure to check back in May when the 2022-2023 game is announced!
